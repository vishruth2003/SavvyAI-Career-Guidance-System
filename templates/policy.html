National Career Service (NCS)

The National Career Service (NCS) connects job seekers with employers and provides career counseling and skill training to reduce skill gaps. It promotes sustainable livelihoods and strengthens India's workforce for better job accessibility.

Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS)

The Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) provides vocational training to disadvantaged groups, including non-literates and school dropouts, focusing on rural areas. It enhances livelihoods and socio-economic conditions through relevant skill development.

Apprenticeship India

The Apprenticeship India Scheme bridges the gap between education and skill development by promoting apprenticeship training across industries. It aims to create a skilled workforce through on-the-job training, boosting employability and productivity.

PMKVY 4.0

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana 4.0 offers short-term training programs to certify millions of youth in industry-relevant skills. Its goal is to boost employability and foster entrepreneurship by equipping individuals with essential skills

Skill India Digital

This initiative provides access to various digital resources and courses aimed at enhancing skills. It seeks to bridge skill gaps across industries by equipping individuals with job-ready skills, improving their employability in the rapidly evolving job market
